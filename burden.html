Burden of your belongings

Clothes, furniture, sport equipment, gifts. Select them wisely.

Every item carries a burden. Storing. Cleaning. Transporting when moving houses.

Feeling bad when not using it. Feeling even worse when getting rid of it.

Cluttering the space of your home.

Draining the wallet.

Temporarily satisfying the thirst for novelty.


Resist.

Think twice. Rent before buying. Prefer non-material gifts. Limit the burden.

Create space.

And enjoy the power.
