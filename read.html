Read like a stoic

What if you will never hold that book again?

What if this is the last time that book is available to you?


A bit sad, isn't it?

But at the same time, do you feel the energy and dedication to fully digest the book?


Squeeze every last drop of information your mind calls for.

Don't let knowledge pass through.

Make it stick: take notes, talk, visualize, write, reread.

Make the book part of your personality.

Make it a unique moment.


Like a place you might never see again...
