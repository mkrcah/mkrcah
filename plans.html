Drop constrains of planning

Plans.

Not goals or ambitions. Our daily plans and expectations.

List of city attractions to see on Saturday.

Required arrival time, because our favorite restaurant closes.

Agenda for a date we push ourselves to follow.


As if we know what our bodies and minds will feel like in future.

Closing ourselves to the opportunities the world offers.

Obliged to follow the predefined plan.


Loose the constraints.

Open yourself to new opportunities.

Hop on the wave of the present.

Like a surfer.

Let your mind and your body dictate the moment.

Embrace the unexpected.

And enjoy the wave.
