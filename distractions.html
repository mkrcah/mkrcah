Choose distractions carefully

With a FitBit tracker, you might not follow your desire and go for a walk instead.

With Instagram, you might miss the moment while trying to impress others with the perfect picture.

With a watch, you allow yourself to be constantly aware of the time.

With mobile notifications, you might ruin the beautiful moment of silence with your best friend.

Just be aware of the cost when adding a new distraction to your day.
